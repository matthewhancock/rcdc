ROCKINGHAM COUNTY DEMOCRATIC COMMITTEE
What We Stand For
The Democratic Party is committed to keeping our nation safe and expanding opportunity for every American. That commitment is reflected in an agenda that emphasizes strong economic growth, affordable health care for all Americans, retirement security, open, honest and accountable government, and securing our nation while protecting our civil rights and liberties.
What We Do
The Rockingham County Democratic Committee works to:
  - Recruit and support Democratic candidates
  - Provide political information to our members and to the public at large
  - Sponsor political events to support and spread the Democratic message
  - Energize Democratic voters to elect our candidates
Meetings
We meet on the second Monday of every month (except December) from 7 to 8PM. Our meetings are open to all Rockingham County Democrats.
